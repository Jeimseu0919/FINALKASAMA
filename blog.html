The rights in terms of data privacy

Posted By Admin    on November 12, 2017   

In 2012 the Philippines passed the Data Privacy Act 2012, comprehensive and strict privacy legislation “to protect the fundamental human right of privacy, of communication while ensuring free flow of information to promote innovation and growth.” (Republic Act. No. 10173, Ch. 1, Sec. 2). This comprehensive privacy law also established a National Privacy Commission that enforces and oversees it and is endowed with rulemaking power. On September 9, 2016, the final implementing rules and regulations came into force, adding specificity to the Privacy Act.

The Data Privacy Act is broadly applicable to individuals and legal entities that process personal information, with some exceptions. The law has extraterritorial application, applying not only to businesses with offices in the Philippines, but when equipment based in the Philippines is used for processing. The act further applies to the processing of the personal information of Philippines citizens regardless of where they reside. One exception in the act provides that the law does not apply to the processing of personal information in the Philippines that was lawfully collected from residents of foreign jurisdictions — an exception helpful for Philippines companies that offer cloud services.

219 is just a number?

Posted By A friendly reminder from SLU Student Court    on December 14,2017  


Bagama’t sa opinyon ng karamihan, na hindi batayan ng pagkatao ang 219, ito naman ang sukatan kung ikaw ay makakapasa sa sa subject mo. Hindi man ito batayan ng talino mo bilang estudyante, na hindi naman talaga, ito naman ang magdidikta kung gaano ka tatagal dito sa San Luis. Dahil hangga’t hindi ka nakakakuha ng 219 o mas higit pa, hindi ka rin makakakuha ng higher subjects na siyang magiging dahilan ng pagka-delay mo. Kaya’t hanggang maari, huwag lang basta 219 ang asamin mo… Mas mataas pa!



Tunay ngang hindi rito nakabase ang dami, lalim, o lawak ng karunungan at kaalaman na nakuha mo rito sa unibersidad, dahil wala naman talagang sukat kung gaano ka natuto o kung hanggang saan ang kaya ng utak mo. Pero huwag mong gawing dahilan ito kapag bumagsak o sa tuwing bumabagsak ka. Na kesyo “It’s just a number. It doesn’t define who I really am.” Ano ba, gumising ka! Dahilan lang ito ng mga taong madaling sumuko, tamad, at hindi kayang ipaglaban ang kung ano ang nararapat para sa kanila. Huwag magpakain sa sistema! Hindi dahil opinyon ito ng karamihan at ito ang na ang nakasanayan, ay ito na ang tama at ito na rin ang magiging prinsipyo mo. Magsumikap ka! Mangarap ka! Umahon ka! Lumangoy ka! At higit sa lahat...Pumasa ka!


You deserve better than this, Louis.

Not just 219, but higher... Better.

Don’t settle for less, if you really deserve the best.

Because 219 is NOT just a number.
