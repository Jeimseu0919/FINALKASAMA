The departamental Exam

Posted By Admin    on November 12, 2017   

On March 23, 2018, the KASAMA/SSC Executive Committee along with selected representatives of recognized students organizations held a dialogue with University President Rev. Fr. Gilbert Sales, CICM and the university Vice Presidents, Mr. Jose Ma. Pangilinan (Academics), Dr. Noel de Leon (Finance), and Atty. Jason Barlis (Administration). One of the topics discussed was the mid-term implementation of the departmental examinations in all schools in which the administration upheld as SLU's transition to standardized teaching and education. As this second semester's final exams near for the Louisians, let us recall the experiences of different students from different schools regarding the recently implemented departmental examinations.
